About Me

I’m a Business Analyst with over 5 years of experience and a strong foundation in analytics and systems transformation. During my MSc in Business Analytics at Dublin Business School, I immersed myself in key disciplines such as Business Intelligence, Applied Statistics, Programming for Analytics, and Project Management.

Through my coursework and practical projects, I gained hands-on experience building dashboards, automating reporting workflows, and applying predictive models to solve real-world business problems. My thesis project, which involved the use of deep learning for food recognition, further solidified my capability to use machine learning in innovative and impactful ways.

In a professional setting, I aim to bring this combined academic and practical knowledge to support data-driven decision-making, streamline operations, and enhance business intelligence.
