AI-Powered Food Recognition Using CNN

My MSc research paper focused on minimizing food wastage in the restaurant industry through an AI-powered food recognition system. I trained and deployed Convolutional Neural Networks (ResNet50 and InceptionV3) using TensorFlow, employing techniques such as EarlyStopping, data augmentation, and custom epoch projection to counter hardware limitations. This system used cameras to identify food items in real-time and update inventory logs automatically, sending alerts before expiration.

Project: Decision Support System for Fraud Detection

As part of my Project Management coursework, I led the design of a Decision Support System (DSS) for fraud detection in financial institutions. The project included a rule-based engine and AI integration for real-time fraud detection with compliance to GDPR and PCI-DSS. I contributed to requirement gathering, stakeholder analysis, risk management, sprint planning using Jira, and resource optimization, resulting in a robust and scalable fraud detection model. This project also involved the use of tools such as Tableau, Python, and AWS.
